ESB Anomaly Detection

Clustering

The purpose of clustering is to analyze different operating modes of HVAC systems in the Engineering Science Building (ESB) at Vanderbilt University.

1. Hourly Clustering

We perform clustering for each hour of a day and analyze the connectivity between clusters of two consecutive hours. A total of 16 features were used, and there are 2529 hourly readings for Fall 2020.
